# AnyBuddy - Social Matching for Wellness & Leisure 🚀

AnyBuddy is a Social Matching App designed to connect people for real-life wellness and leisure experiences. Unlike dating apps, AnyBuddy focuses on "doing life together" through shared interests and built-in logistics.

## 🌟 Key Features
* **Quick Match Questionnaire:** Dynamic quiz to capture leisure preferences (Genre, Trip Type, etc.) and availability.
* **Smart Matching:** Relevance scoring to prioritize profiles based on shared interests and location.
* **Dedicated Chat:** Integrated communication to foster authentic connections and plan activity execution.

## 📊 Project Management & Strategy (PM Perspective)

### Market Opportunity
AnyBuddy addresses a critical gap in the Israeli market: the lack of a single platform uniting niche social connections (Parties, Nutrition, Fitness) with built-in activity logistics.

### Core User Stories
* **UX/UI & Personalization:** As a user, I want to select specific activities (e.g., concerts, hiking) so the app can personalize my matches.
* **Location-Based Logic:** As a user, I want to see matches available in my area to ensure real-world meeting feasibility.

### Risk Management
* **Technical:** Algorithm accuracy optimization via continuous beta testing and user feedback.
* **Privacy & Security:** Implementation of end-to-end encryption standards for chat safety.

### Business KPIs
* **Conversion:** Targeted 30% Match-to-Activity conversion rate by Q4.
* **Retention:** 35% 30-day post-event retention target.
